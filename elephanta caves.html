Location

Elephanta Caves are located on Elephanta Island, about 11 km from Mumbai, Maharashtra.

How to Go

  • By Ferry: Take a ferry from the Gateway of India, Mumbai. The ride takes around one hour.

Requirements

  • Comfortable walking shoes for exploring caves.
  • Water bottles and light snacks.
  • Cash for entry tickets and shopping.

Food Junctions Nearby

  • Cannon Pav Bhaji – Famous for local street food.
  • Elephanta Island Cafeteria – Offers snacks and beverages.

Review

Elephanta Caves is a UNESCO World Heritage Site known for its rock-cut temples dedicated to Lord Shiva. Best visited during winter for pleasant weather.

Suitable Places for Current Season

  • Great Cave – Main attraction featuring Trimurti sculpture.
  • Stupa Hill – A trekking spot offering scenic views.

Description

Elephanta Caves showcase ancient Indian art and culture with intricate carvings dating back to the 5th century. The island is a serene getaway from Mumbai's busy life, offering history, spirituality, and adventure.
